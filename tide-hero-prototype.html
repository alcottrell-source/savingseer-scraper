What the diet actually does

  • Verb leads, number supports. "Go now / Easing / Rising" is the decision; the % is the evidence under it. One thing to read, not eight.
  • The unit is welded on. "59% of shops on sale" — never a bare "59%" that reads as a discount.
  • Direction lives in one place. The trend ("up from 41%") sits under the fact in A, or in the line itself in B — no duplicate arrows, pills and chips fighting.
  • The lens is demoted, not deleted. Your-shops vs whole-centre becomes a footer control (A) or a single line (B) — present, never competing with the headline.
  • It's unmistakably Tide. Playfair italic verb, neon waterline, gold "go", crest marker — a point of view, not a template hero.
